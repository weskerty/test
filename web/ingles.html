🛂 RULES

As in any community, there are rules here to live in harmony. Although each group/channel has its own rules, these are the main ones;

🔞 No Minors Allowed. There are +18 Groups/Channels in the Community.
💡 No Sensitive Multimedia Sensitive images are those that may disturb some people due to depictions of violence, suffering, sex, or any other content that may be upsetting or stressful. Although there are Groups/Channels where +18 content is allowed.
💭 No Spam in the Group or Private Messages. Spam refers to unwanted messages, usually for commercial or advertising purposes.
💱 No Pyramid Schemes or Scams. We don't want to be our own bosses; we don't want Cryptos.
🚯 Respect the Freedoms of Others. Many people can't tolerate others having ideologies different from their own and end up fighting. Let's respect each other's freedom to think and say what they want.
